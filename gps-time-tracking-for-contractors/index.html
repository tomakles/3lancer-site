Frequently Asked Questions

How does GPS time tracking work?

You set a project location (address). When you enter that area, the app can start tracking or remind you to start. It uses your phone's GPS to detect when you are on-site.

Does Baxilancer track location all the time?

No. It only checks your location to see if you are at a job site you defined. Your privacy is important, and location data is used solely for time tracking.

Is Baxilancer suitable for small contractors?

Yes! It is perfect for independent contractors, electricians, plumbers, and small crews who need a simple way to track hours without paperwork.

Can I export reports for invoicing?

Absolutely. You can generate detailed reports of your hours and expenses, which you can use to create accurate invoices for your clients.
