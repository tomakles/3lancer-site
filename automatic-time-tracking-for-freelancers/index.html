Frequently Asked Questions

What does automatic time tracking mean?

It means the app tracks your work hours based on your location or schedule without you having to manually start and stop a timer every time.

Can I track expenses with this app?

Yes, Baxilancer includes AI receipt scanning so you can track expenses and link them directly to your projects.

Is this suitable for freelancers with multiple clients?

Absolutely. You can set up multiple projects for different clients and the app will track time separately for each one.

How do I get reports for taxes?

The app generates detailed reports of your hours and expenses, which you can export and use for your tax filings or send to your accountant.
