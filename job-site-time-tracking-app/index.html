Frequently Asked Questions

How does GPS tracking work on job sites?

You define the job site location in the app. When you arrive, the app detects it via GPS and starts tracking your time automatically.

Can I track multiple sites in one day?

Yes. Baxilancer is designed for multi-site days. It will log your time at each location separately, giving you a clear timeline of your day.

Does it track driving time?

Yes, the app can track the time spent driving between job sites, ensuring you capture all billable hours.

Can I attach receipts to specific jobs?

Absolutely. You can scan receipts and link them to the specific job or project they belong to, keeping your expenses organized.
